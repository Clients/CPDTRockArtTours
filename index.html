We are based in Rangely Co and are apart of the Uinta Basin and the Dinosaur Diamond. The tours will take you back in time to a place, not so long ago, where Native Indians once lived before that land gave way to family farms and one-room schoolhouses.

Your guide is one of a few folks left that have personal stories about growing up in the Colorado wild west. She will tell you stories, behind the petroglyphs and ghost towns, that have been pass down through the ages around campfires and thanksgiving tables. Stories that come from personal family history of the area and growing up in rural America.

Learn the unique hidden history of Colorado.

Canyon Pintado (Painted Canyon) has been occupied by prehistoric people for as long as 11,000 years and was repeatedly visited by a variety of cultures throughout its long history.

Head out on Dragon Trail to local ghost towns, that were once thriving cities, and you will see petroglyphs that have endured thousands of years and hold a history of their own.

For the adventure inside us all there is a quite place tucked away in mountias of Rangely Colorado that will take you back in time to a place filled with the spirit of the America West.
