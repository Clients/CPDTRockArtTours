Cost: $150 per person (Discount for Vets) - Lunch and Drinks included

Your tour will start out at the Rangely Outdoor Musemn and end at the Rangely Automotive Musemn to bring you full circle from a time when native indians hunted this land to modern man hunting this land. Your guide is one of a few folks left that have personal stories about growing up in the Colorado wild west. She will tell you stories, behind the petroglyphs and ghost towns, that have been pass down through the ages around campfires and thanksgiving tables. Stories that come from personal family history of the area and growing up in rural America.
