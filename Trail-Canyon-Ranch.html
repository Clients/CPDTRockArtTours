Trail Canyon Ranch has been known to hunters as a tranquile place to rest their head after a long day of hunting.

Others have come to this place to work, play or relax. Just outside the city limits we are remote enough to operate off of solar power, wind, and generater. Phone service is limited and we do not have WIFI. Thoses that come here are looking to put aside the stress of city life and step back into a simpler lifestyle.

Here you will find your way back to nature and reconect with your soul.

The ranch offers season round activities such as:

  • Snow shoeing
  • Cross coutry skiing
  • Snowmobiling (must bring your own)
  • Horseback riding
  • Mountian bicking
  • Site Seeing
  • Hicking
  • Fishing
  • Hunting

If you are looking for a place to stay that is outside of the ordinary please call (970) 657-3033 for available times and bookings.
